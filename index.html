Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our workshops and programs.

Who can attend these workshops? expand_more
Our workshops are designed for a wide range of individuals including students, working professionals, educators, and anyone passionate about personal and professional growth. Most of our programs are beginner-friendly unless stated otherwise.
Do I receive a certificate upon completion? expand_more
Yes, all participants who successfully complete a workshop will receive a verified certificate of participation from the International Human Rights Council - Maharashtra Board.
Will I get a refund after registering? expand_more
Registrations for our workshops are generally non-refundable as the proceeds are immediately allocated towards organizing the event and funding our public welfare initiatives. However, if a workshop is canceled or rescheduled by the IHRC Maharashtra Board, you will be eligible for a full refund.
How does IHRC support public welfare? expand_more
The IHRC Maharashtra Board uses funds from workshop registrations to drive multiple public welfare campaigns. This includes supporting rural education, organizing health and nutrition camps, providing legal aid, and empowering underprivileged communities through essential skill development programs.
