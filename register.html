AI & ChatGPT

This workshop is designed to help you understand how AI and ChatGPT can be used in real life. From writing and research to productivity and problem solving, you will learn how to use these tools effectively.

The session focuses on practical use cases so that you can apply what you learn immediately in your studies, work, or personal projects.
